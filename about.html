Internet of Things

What is IoT?

The Internet of Things (IoT) is the network of physical objects that are embedded with sensors, software, and other technologies for the purpose of connecting and exchanging data with other devices and systems over the internet.

IoT devices can collect and transmit data about their environment, such as temperature, humidity, motion, and location. This data can then be used to improve efficiency, automate tasks, and make better decisions.

Common IoT Sensors

  • Temperature sensor

    Temperature sensors measure the temperature of their environment.

  • Humidity sensor

    Humidity sensors measure the amount of moisture in the air.

  • Motion sensor

    Motion sensors detect movement.

  • Location sensor

    Location sensors track the location of an object.
